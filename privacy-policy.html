1. Intro

Your privacy is a big deal to us. This explains:
* How we collect info about you.
* What we do with it and who sees it.
* What you can do regarding your info.
* How we keep your info safe.

This applies no matter where you are in the world.

2. What Info We Grab

We get info in a few ways: straight from you, automatically when you use our stuff, and sometimes from other companies.

a. Info You Give Us

*   Basics: Like your name, email, number, address.
*   Account stuff: Username, password, what you like.
*   Payment stuff: Where to send the bill and how you pay (it's safe).
*   What you tell us: Feedback, survey answers, other messages.
*   Job stuff: If you apply to work here.

b. Info We Get Automatically

*   Device and browser info: IP address, what kind of browser you use, your device's ID.
*   How you use our stuff: What pages you look at, where you click, how long you stay, where you came from.
*   Where you are: Roughly, or exactly if you let us.
*   Cookies and other tracking things.

c. Info From Other Companies

*   If you sign in with Google or something else.
*   Advertising partners.
*   Companies we work with, or what's public.

3. How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

*   Keep things running: Give you our products and help.
*   Make things better: See how people use our stuff and make it easier to use.
*   Talk to you: Send you updates, emails about your account, and marketing stuff (if you said it was okay).
*   Take payments: Handle orders and subscriptions.
*   Keep things safe: Stop bad guys, and protect our stuff.
*   Follow the law: Taxes and other legal stuff.
*   Good business reasons: Like research and making new stuff.

4. Who We Share Your Info With

We only share info when we need to:

*   Companies that help us: Like hosting, payment, email, and support.
*   Our sister companies: So we can all work together.
*   If we get bought or merge with someone else.
*   If the law makes us: To answer legal requests or protect people.
*   If you say it's okay.

We don't sell your info.

5. Cookies

Cookies help us know who you are when you come back, remember what you like, and see if things are working. You can turn them off in your browser, but some things might not work right.

6. How Long We Keep Your Info

We only keep your info as long as it's needed for what we said here, or as the law says. It depends on what kind of info it is.

7. How We Protect your Data

We have security to protect your info, like passwords, locks, and checking things regularly. Nothing online is 100% safe, so use our stuff knowing that.

8. Where Your Info Might Go

Your info might go to other countries, like the USA. We use legal protection when that happens.

9. Your Rights

You might be able to:

*   See your info.
*   Fix wrong info.
*   Delete your info.
*   Stop us from using your info in certain ways.
*   Take back your permission for us to use your info.
*   Say no to targeted ads.

10. California and Other US State Rights

Under California law, you can:

*   Ask what info we've collected.
*   Ask us to delete your info.
*   Say no to us sharing your info.
*   Have someone else make these requests for you.

We won't treat you badly if you use these rights.

11. Europe and UK Rights

Under European and UK law, you have the rights listed in Section 9. We use your permission, contracts, the law, and our own good reasons to use your info.

You can contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at \[DPO email] or complain to your local privacy authority.

12. Kids' Privacy

Our stuff is not for kids under 13. We don't collect their info. If we find out we have it, we'll delete it.

13. Links to Other Sites

We might link to other websites. We are not in charge of their privacy, so read their rules.

14. Changes

We might change this sometimes. We’ll tell you on our website or directly. If you keep using our stuff after that, you’re agreeing to the new rules.
